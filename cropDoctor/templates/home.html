Why is this project made?

Many farmers suffer without prior knowledge on how to treat different types of plants which suffer from various kinds of diseases.

Our project is made delve and solve on that specific problem

The CropDoctor project is made using a Deep Learning model which is based on Residual Neural Networks(ResNet).

How to use our project?

  • Scan the leaf with a phone camera.
  • Upload the image in the box given below.
  • Click on "Detect" button.
  • You will be redirected to a Wikipedia page.
  • Here you will know everything about the disease detected.
  • You will also find neccesary steps to cure and prevent the disease.
